model finishing school

The Model Finishing School is a pioneering venture of the Government of Kerala,
, set up with the purpose of enhancing employability of qualified youths from the State of Kerala.A Government of Kerala (GoK) undertaking, the Model Finishing School is a joint initiative of the IT Department, GoK and the Institute of Human Resources Development (IHRD), with the support of industry-major Infosys. The IHRD is responsible for the day-to-day functioning of the institution
